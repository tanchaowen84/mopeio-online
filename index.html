About Mope.io

Mope.io is an exciting multiplayer survival game where you start as a small animal and work your way up the food chain. Hunt other players, gather food, and evolve into stronger creatures as you progress through the game.

How to Play

  • • Use mouse or touch controls to move your animal
  • • Eat food and smaller animals to gain XP
  • • Avoid larger animals that can eat you
  • • Level up to evolve into stronger creatures
  • • Use special abilities to survive and hunt
